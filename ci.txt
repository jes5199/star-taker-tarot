十二次 — 12 Cì (Chinese Jupiter Stations)

Jupiter takes ~12 years to orbit the sky; each cì is the segment it occupies
for roughly one year.  Boundaries align with xiù boundaries (hour-circle
projection onto the ecliptic).  Positions for 2030.

3    娵訾 Jūzī              Juzi, who sighs      室・壁            17°38' Pisces
4    降婁 Jiànglóu          Drawing downward     奎・婁            13°33' Aries
5    大梁 Dàliáng           Great Beam           胃・昴・畢         08°53' Taurus

8    鶉火 Chúnhuǒ           Quail Fire           柳・星・張         12°15' Leo

10   壽星 Shòuxīng          Longevity Star       角・亢            20°03' Libra
