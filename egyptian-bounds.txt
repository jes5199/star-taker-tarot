Egyptian Bounds (Terms) — from Ptolemy's Tetrabiblos I.21

Each sign is divided into 5 unequal segments, each ruled by a classical
planet (excluding Sun and Moon).  These are the "Egyptian" terms as
transmitted by Ptolemy, the system used in Hellenistic and medieval practice.
